  - **Instructor:** [Saravanan Vijayakumaran](http://www.ee.iitb.ac.in/~sarva)

  - Bitcoin
  - Ethereum
  - Stellar
  - Monero
  - zkSNARKs
  - Hyperledger Fabric
  - Quorum
  - Corda
  - Blockstack
  - IPFS
